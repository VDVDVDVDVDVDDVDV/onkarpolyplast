About Us

Onkar Polyplast Private Limited

Onkar Polyplast Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer & supplier of Rigid PVC & HDPE Pipes, Sprinkler Irregation System, ASTM Pipe, SWR Pipes & Fitting and Suction & Garden Tubing. Onkar Polyplast Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1991. For the past 30 years of our existence, our steadfast focus has been providing BEST QUALITY HDPE Pipes and Irregation System at competitive prices.

These products are designed with the use of best quality materials. Our products are appreciated for their features like longer life, easy to use, sturdy designs, crack resistance, high strength, and lightweight. Additionally, as per the diverse needs of the patrons, these products are also available in both standards as well as in modified options.

We are supported by a spacious infrastructure facility. We have installed highly advanced tools and machinery in our manufacturing unit. Also, we are divided into different teams that take care of all the processes of designing, manufacture, quality check, and control. In addition to this, the proficiency of all our teams and their business endeavors help us in bringing out a range of excellent quality products.
